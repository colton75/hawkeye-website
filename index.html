About

Hawkeye, your daily driver

HAWKEYE Smart Mirror provides an access point for a person to receive all the information that could affect how they plan for the day. All the necessary information like weather and news can be accessed from one location.

All the features you need

HAWKEYE Smart Mirror is a fully featured mirror that you can control with voice, gesture and companion app. It has speakers, camera, microphone, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. It is splash-proof and moisture resistant.

And many more...

Features

Personal Assistant

Alexa built in enables you to control your mirror with voice commands.

Home Intrusion System

Keeps your home safe by alerting you when there is an intruder in your home.

Integrates with smart devices

Compatible with smart devices from leading brands. Control your smart devices with our built in alexa.

Motion activated

It goes to sleep when you are not using it and wakes up when you come back. This feature makes the mirror power efficient.

App remote control

Control your mirror with our companion app available on play store and apple app store.

Highly Customizable

Customize the mirror to see only those things that matter to you. Select from a wide variety of apps available on our store to install and start using.

App

Companion App

The Hawkeye app gives you the convinence to control your smart mirror from your smartphone. You can manage installed apps, install new apps, manage settings of the mirror and many more. You will receive notification along with live video feed on the app if there is any intrusion taking place at your home.
